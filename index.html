Hunter’s Keeps The Ones Abafostayo Refreshed.

In the true spirit of Uk’fosta, Hunter’s was introduced to Mzansi back in 1988, as the first original premium cider to launch, in a beer drinking market. Though the challenge was tough, through persistence, hard work and Uk’fosta, Hunter’s managed to grow from newcomer to being loved and accepted as a true Mzansi original.

That is a story of Uk’fosta. A story that many South Africans who wake up day after day, after day to fetch their dreams can relate with. Hunter’s celebrates this never-give-up spirit.
That’s why Hunter’s keeps the ones Abafostayo refreshed.
