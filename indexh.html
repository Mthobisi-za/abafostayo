Hunter’s Keeps The
One’s Abafostayo Refreshed.

In the true spirit of uk’fosta, Hunter’s was introduced to Mzansi back in 1988, as the first premium cider to launch, in a beer drinking market. Though the challenge was tough, through persistence, hard work and uk’fosta, Hunter’s managed to grow from newcomer to being loved and accepted as a true Mzansi original.

That is a story of uk’fosta. A story that many South Africans who wake up day after day, after day to fetch their dreams can relate with. Hunter’s celebrates this never-give-up spirit.
That’s why Hunter’s keeps the one’s Aba’fostayo refreshed.
